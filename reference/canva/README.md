## Files

- `page.html`: published Canva page response, including the serialized design bootstrap data.

- `fonts.css`: linked Canva Sans stylesheet `_assets/static_font_4.ltr.css`.

- `manifest.json`: media-to-section mapping, dimensions, and source URLs.
- `content.md`: normalized visible copy extracted from the published design.

The snapshot does not include Canva JavaScript runtimes, footer resources, hidden-page-only media, or font binaries. The CSS and HTML may therefore not render as a standalone offline copy. Their purpose is to let future agents inspect the generated structure, styles, identifiers, and source composition.

## Restrictions

- Do not hotlink the Canva URLs in production.
- Do not serve this directory directly.
- Confirm reuse rights for photographs, cutouts, map art, Canva elements, and fonts before deployment.
- Prefer original files supplied by the couple or photographer over these processed derivatives.
- `watermarked: false` in Canva metadata does not establish ownership or redistribution rights.

## Published Design Notes

- The Canva design uses a 1366 by 768 coordinate system and absolute positioning.
- Twelve sections are published; five alternate/reference design records are hidden.
- The design uses Playfair Display, Garet, Snell Roundhand, Apricots, and possibly Arimo in addition to Canva runtime fonts.
- Do not derive font licensing rights from the Canva stylesheet. Obtain licensed webfont files or approve substitutes.
